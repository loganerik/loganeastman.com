title: "The Projectionist"
date: 2026-03-10
postType: poem
draft: true
description: "A poem about light, dark rooms, and the stories we tell ourselves."
---

He threads the film through teeth of light,
each frame a promise kept to no one.
The audience shifts. The reel complains.
He doesn't watch the screen anymore —

he watches the dust.

How it catches the beam halfway down,
suspended like a thought
you haven't finished thinking.
The hero dies. The hero lives.
It's all the same machinery.

Outside, the marquee buzzes
its one good vowel into the rain.
He locks up, walks home,
already forgetting the ending —

which is, of course,
the only honest review.
